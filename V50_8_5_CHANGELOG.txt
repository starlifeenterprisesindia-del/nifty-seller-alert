NIFTY SELLER AI V50.8.5 — INSTITUTIONAL JOURNAL + SHORT-HORIZON OUTLOOK
Date: 14-07-2026
Base: V50.8.4 DSP Evidence Integrity

1) FII/DII protected journal
- Storage raised from 30 to 60 trading-day rows.
- Every save is mirrored atomically to data/fii_dii_journal.csv,
  .runtime_state/fii_dii_journal.csv and .runtime_state/fii_dii_journal.json.
- Existing rows are merged by Date; a shorter browser/session copy cannot
  replace a longer history.
- Missing fields do not erase previously saved fields.
- Session/shared-state records are merged on load.
- One-click Clear Journal was removed.
- Verify/Repair Storage and safe CSV backup merge were added.
- 5D, 10D, 15D and 30D institutional totals are available internally.
- Missing institutional days remain unavailable, not artificial zero values.

Important storage boundary:
Local/runtime mirrors protect browser reconnects and normal app reruns while
that deployment filesystem remains available. Streamlit redeployment can
replace its filesystem. Keep the downloadable CSV backup and use Restore/Merge
CSV after a redeploy unless a future external database is configured.

2) Short-Horizon Market Prediction table
- New separate table directly below AI FINAL AUTHORITY.
- Primary horizon: next 15 minutes.
- Confirmation horizon: next 30 minutes.
- Shows UP %, DOWN %, RANGE %, dominant outcome, reliability and ATR-based zone.
- Percentages always total 100.
- Read-only SHADOW / INFORMATION ONLY architecture.
- Runs only after AI_MASTER final judgement and uses the same snapshot evidence.
- Cannot change action, execution permission, strategy, strike, SL or target.
- Stale quote/option chain, OI hold, critical DSP integrity failure,
  insufficient samples or movement continuity gap makes outlook unavailable.
- Opening and lunch zones cap reliability.
- One official forecast is stored per five-minute bucket.
- 15m/30m outcomes are evaluated only near the requested time window;
  late observations are marked MISSED_WINDOW rather than counted as accuracy.
- Validation remains PROVISIONAL until enough completed cases exist.

3) PDF and state integration
- The app PDF now includes the Short-Horizon Prediction section.
- FII/DII journal records and shadow forecast cases are included in bounded
  runtime-state merge keys.
- V50.8.4 DSP integrity and option-flow corrections remain intact.
