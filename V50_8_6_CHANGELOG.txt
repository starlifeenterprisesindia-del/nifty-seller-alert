NEW MARKET PATH FORECAST TABLE
- Added a separate MARKET PATH FORECAST table directly below AI FINAL AUTHORITY.
- Next 15 Minutes is the primary forecast.
- Next 30 Minutes is the confirmation forecast.
- The table shows:
  * current Nifty
  * verified support and resistance with source
  * market bias
  * UP / DOWN / RANGE probabilities
  * expected remaining move in points
  * likely destination zone
  * stretch zone
  * invalidation level
  * reversal risk
  * move consumed percentage
  * reliability
  * most likely path
  * forecast status

PATH BEHAVIOUR LABELS
- UP -> CONTINUATION
- UP -> STALL / BREAKOUT TEST
- UP -> REVERSAL RISK
- DOWN -> CONTINUATION
- DOWN -> SUPPORT / BREAKDOWN TEST
- DOWN -> SUPPORT BOUNCE RISK
- RANGE -> UPSIDE BREAK TEST
- RANGE -> DOWNSIDE BREAK TEST
- TWO-WAY WHIPSAW / RANGE

ONE-BRAIN AUTHORITY LOCK
- Forecast runs only after final AI_MASTER judgement.
- AI_MASTER and market snapshot are passed as deep copies.
- Forecast performs zero independent market-data fetches.
- Direction starts from the final AI_MASTER projection.
- Live movement can only make a bounded timing adjustment.
- Support/resistance can cap zones and raise reversal risk, but cannot invent a
  separate direction.
- Forecast cannot issue BUY, SELL CE, SELL PE or IRON CONDOR.
- Forecast cannot choose strike, hedge, SL or target.
- Forecast cannot modify action, execution status, confidence, strategy scores,
  candidates or plans.
- App compares locked AI_MASTER fields before and after forecast creation. Any
  detected mutation discards the forecast and keeps the final judgement safe.
- The UI displays One-Brain Authority Lock PASS/HOLD.

SAFETY AND FRESHNESS
- Forecast is unavailable when market is closed, quote/option-chain is stale,
  OI sync is not ready, movement continuity resets, recent samples are
  insufficient, critical DSP integrity fails or snapshot IDs do not match.
- Opening shock and lunch-zone reliability remain capped.
- Unvalidated forecasts are reliability-capped to prevent false certainty:
  * under 20 completed cases: 15m max 62%, 30m max 56%
  * under 50 completed cases: 15m max 70%, 30m max 64%
  * under 100 completed cases: 15m max 78%, 30m max 72%
- A forecast cannot show CONFIRMED PATH during the initial provisional stage.
